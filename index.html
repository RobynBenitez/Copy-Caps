About

Robyn grew up not knowing what he wanted to be when he grow up, young Robyn toyed with the idea of becoming a lawyer. He admired his mother's dedication to justice and her ability to argue her point, but before he delved into the world of law, he realized that the intricacies of legal codes and lengthy documents were not his cup of tea. However, he did have a small but growing passion for engineering. The way machines and structures worked, the tangible aspect of creating something from scratch fascinated him. But, as fate would have it, he couldn't escape the fact that engineering is quite expensive and often required dealing with complex mathematical equations. This presented a frustrating hurdle for him, as numbers seemed like an insurmountable obstacle. Robyn's journey took an unexpected turn as he prioritized the desire to complete his education swiftly and start earning money. He didn't have a particular affinity for computers or any strong passion for a specific career. His primary goal was to finish school as soon as possible and enter the workforce. In this pursuit of practicality, he found that computer science offered a fast track to a well-paying job, even though he didn't have a deep-rooted interest in the field. This decision allowed him to meet wonderful people and secure his financial future.
